A quick intro to user experience

User-centered design places users at the center of the design process. The focus is on helping users meet needs, breaking down complex challenges into manageable parts, and fostering memorable, meaningful, and impactful experiences.

The following pages will share some practices and tools our team uses in the user-centered design process, along with an example of how they were used in an actual project.
