Key Ideas for Design

In this mini-course, we take a practical approach to design theory by connecting the theories, which we call "key ideas," with your real life experiences.

In this section, we will introduce some learning theories to explore several key ideas related to design.

Then, you'll connect those key ideas with your own experiences and share those experiences with your team.
