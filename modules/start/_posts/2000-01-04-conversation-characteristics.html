What makes memorable, meaningful, and transformative design?

In this part of the activity, you'll turn your experiences into a list of characteristics of memorable, meaningful, and transformative design. Over time, you'll develop your list to explore how to make your designs more memorable, meaningful, and transformative.

{% include icon-team.html %}With your team: Characteristics of Effective Design

Part 1

Think about the stories shared in the previous activity. Work with your team to brainstorm a list of the characteristics of design you noticed in these stories. It might help you to think about how you would complete the following sentences for design:

Design for UX:

Design is most memorable when: ______________.
Design is most meaningful when: ______________.
Design is most transformative when: ______________.

Tips

  • Try completing the phrases from multiple perspectives, including the designers, various user groups, and motivational/contextual factors.

Part 2

Next, share what your wrote with your teammates. Talk together with your team about the following questions:

  • What themes or categories are you noticing?
  • How can we balance seemingly contradictory ideas?
  • What are the most important ideas for you as a designer and creator?
  • What are the most important ideas for you as a user?
